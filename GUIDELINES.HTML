• Please find below some guidelines to maintain the decorum of the hospital and its premises. We understand that your relatives and friends are important part of your life and recovery process.

    • Strictly follow visiting time and restrict your visitors during visiting hours for the comfort of all patients.

    Our visiting hours are:

  • 4:00 pm to 6:30 pm on all days ICU – 9:00 am – 9:30 am & 5:00 pm – 6:30 pm


  • You will be issued passes for attendants and visitors at the time of admission of patient. hence we request visitors to wear passes provided at the entrance in the hospital premises.


  • Children below the age of 12 years are prohibited as visitors as they are more susceptible to infections. They are allowed on request on Saturdays and Sundays.
  • It is advised to keep yourself sterilized during hospital visit.
    Sanitize your hands before and after every visit.


  • Use of mobile phones is prohibited where critical medical equipment and patient monitoring systems are in operation.
    If you must use your cell phone in approved areas, please speak in a soft tone so that other patients are not disturbed
    • .
  • Flowers can be a source of infection and hence discouraged in the Hospital.


  • Taking photos, videos or making sound recordings without permission is strictly prohibited on the Hospital premises.


  • Smoking & Alcohol consumption is strictly prohibited on the hospital premises.


  • The entire hospital premises is under CCTV surveillance.


  • Being a public place, patients are strongly advised not to keep valuable items/cash in their room/ward. The Hospital will not be responsible for any loss/damage of your personal belongings anywhere within the Hospital premises.
